HAPPINESS

Happiness is a profound state of inner joy, contentment, and satisfaction that transcends material possessions. It is not a final destination but rather a daily choice and a way of living that can be nurtured through gratitude, positive thinking, and meaningful relationships.
While often sought in success or wealth, true happiness is usually found in the simple, small moments of life—like sharing a laugh with family, enjoying nature, or helping someone in need.
