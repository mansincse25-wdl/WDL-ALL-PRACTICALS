Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was great and brave ruler of India and the founder of the Maratha Empire. He was born on 19 February 1630 and is remembered for his courage, intelligence, and leadership. Shivaji Maharaj created a strong and disciplined army and introduced innovative military strategies.

He respected all religions and worked for the welfare of his people. His forts, administration, and navy made his kingdom powerful. Shivaji Maharaj is known for his honesty, bravery, and determination to establish Swarajya.
